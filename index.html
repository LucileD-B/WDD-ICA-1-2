Adventure begins here!

Skiing lessons

The best way to spend a fun, no-holds-barred week in the mountains!

Accompanied by an instructor, you can ski with friends, progress according to your level, and discover new horizons.

On the first day, the instructors form groups by level and age. The groups are progressive according to the progression of each person.

If you're a beginner

Never been on skis before? This is your chance to get on skis in a fun and relaxed atmosphere. You will be able to familiarize yourself with skiing through specific workshops:
- small bumps
- direct tracks with obstacles, etc.

Your challenge? To find solutions to avoid falling and to position yourself on the skis well.
You will quickly make your first turns on the prepared course:
- poles, cones, hoops,
- raised bumps

If you've skied for a week before...

You will be able to progress on a reserved track with workshops recreating the difficulties encountered on the slopes:
- bumps,
- slalom layouts,
- hoops,
- mini springboard
Altering each day, your instructor will take you on the blue slopes of the area and will teach you the safety rules.

If you're are an intermediate skier...

Can you manage well on skis? You'll be able to extend your field of action, strengthen your technique and assert your autonomy by alternating training in the resort's slalom stadiums and discovering the Avoriaz and Portes du Soleil areas.
On the program :
- Picnic outings,
- Playful sessions,
- Jumping or parallel slalom competitions,
- Skiing in all types of snow,
- Big curves in powder,
- Test run training.

Speed & perfecting

Build your champion spirit!

Our competition lessons allow those who have just got their Gold Star to continue to make progress in a competition context.

Competition training courses are proposed to those who already have their "Flèche d'Argent" and who want to take part in more intense sessions. They are supervised by instructors who are specialised in competition: coaches, "Chamois" openers, finalists of the instructors' challenge, former high-level athletes.

The various events :

The Flèche

Giant Slalom event, a course with between 25 and 35 gates and a vertical drop of 200 to 250m.

For even more thrills

TEAM RIDER, all-terrain ski fun...

If you've got the gold star and you'd rather learn all-terrain skiing than racing, the team rider course is for you!

Team Rider courses offer you more fun skiing with moguls, off-piste, powder, snowpark, all-terrain skiing, all-snow skiing, arva initiation, etc.
You'll make new friends around your own age and share a fun moments with our passionate instructors.

So, come and learn and enjoy the spirit of our mountains, and ride in the Avoriaz and Portes du Soleil area!

FREESTYLE SKIING TRAINING COURSES, discovery or improvement...

Available to teenagers from the "Flèche de Bronze" level, the Freestyle Skiing training courses are supervised by qualified instructors and allow you to discover freestyle skiing or to improve your skills in the various areas of Avoriaz.
On the program :
- Big Air,
- Half pipe,
- Skiercross,
- Rails,
- Box,
- Back country session (depending on the snow conditions).
Enjoy all this in a fun and safe atmosphere!

Nature Escape

Portes du Soleil, expérience

Discover the slopes of the Portes du soleil area or off-piste skiing and live an unforgettable experience in a group of good skiers or snowboarders, guided by a qualified instructor.
Depending on the snow and weather conditions, the trip in the Portes du Soleil can be modified.

Vallée Blanche, mythical route

Explore this mythical high mountain route, with over 2000m of descent!
Enjoy this experience with your family or friends in a wonderful environment in groups of 2 to 8 people, supervised by a guide.
Only suitable for good skiers.
Safety equipment provided by the guide (harness and avalanche transceiver).
